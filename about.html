COMMON TYPES OF CYBERCRIMES

COMMON TYPES OF CYBERCRIMINAL

PHISHERS-Are dishonest individuals who trick or deceive people, often for financial gain. They use various tactics to defraud or manipulate others, such as fake offers, schemes, or impersonation.

CYBER-ATTACKS COUNTERMEASURES

NETWORK ENCRYPTION-security technique that protects data as it travels over a computer network. It scrambles the information so that it can only be understood by the intended recipient, making it difficult for unauthorized parties to intercept and read the data.
